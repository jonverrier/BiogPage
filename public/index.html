Professional background.

Systems

Most of my career has involved leading the specification & delivery of large scale systems in Financial Services. For most of the last decade I have been the technology lead for Accenture Post Trade Processing, a cloud-based back office service for Investment Banks, for which we had three large banks as clients. Earlier in my career I led the build of Clearstream's intra-day securities settlement system, and also led the build of the BACS/VocaLink online payments system. I operate across the full lifecycle, and the full breadth of systems required by large enterprises.

Business

As a business leader in APTP & in the wider firm, I helped sell & deliver engagements of 10s-100s of $M. I have also worked with clients on the front end of new venture creation - for example I worked with the corporate development team on the origination of the Egg Invest online mutual fund supermarket.

Technology

I have built, and retain, a strong 'full stack' technology skill set. In my early years at Intelligent Environments I worked on an expert system, and built highly technical components such as a dynamic linker. I was lucky enough to work on the very first versions of Windows & Windows NT, programming in C & C++, all of which went on to be broadly adopted in the IT industry, and gave me a career leg up on the way. My C++, Typescript, & Web skills are up to date, with some sample project including this website on my GitHub page.
